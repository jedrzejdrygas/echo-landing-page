Based in Wrocław, Poland, PROJECT ECHO is a dynamic mix of DJs and visual artists. Each artist offers a specific style but in unison, we offer a unique blend of musical performances to rock your venue. We combine audio with the visual, offering both DJ, as well as VJ coverage. The complete party package.
